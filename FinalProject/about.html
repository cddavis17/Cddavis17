Welcome to The Elder Scrolls About Page!

The purpose of this page is to list and talk about The Elder Scrolls games in the order that they were released. This is an out of universe page that discusses the themes of the games and how they play. My opinion on the games is somewhat biased towards the newer, more modern and accessible games over the old style. On the side is the list of the games in chronological order.

  • The Elder Scrolls: Arena (1994)
  • The Elder Scrolls II: Daggerfall (1996)
  • An Elder Scrolls Legend: Battlespire (1997)
  • The Elder Scrolls Adventure: Redguard (1998)
  • The Elder Scrolls III: Morrowind (2002)
  • The Elder Scrolls IV: Oblivion (2006)
  • The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim (2011)
  • The Elder Scrolls Online (2014)
  • The Elder Scrolls: Blades (2019)

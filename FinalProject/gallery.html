Welcome to The Elder Scrolls Gallery!

The Elder Scrolls Gallery page is intended to show off as many of Nirn's intriguing creatures and as much of the beauties of the world of Nirn as possible. The following gallery consists of creatures and beings from all corners of existence, from oblivion to mundus and beyond.The gallery starts out with two dragon priests named Ahzidal and Volsung, ancient undead mages who once ruled Skyrim under the watchful eye of their dragon lords. The third slide shows a corrupted shade, a ghost whose spirit is corrupted and hostile. Slide 4 shows a minor daedric creature that resembles a dinosaur, the clannfear. The next slide is a spriggan, a corrupted spirit of the woods. Slide 6 shows a storm atronach, a daedric creature that can be summoned by those with good enough conjuration skill to fight alongside you in mundus. Slide 7 shows a more prestigious daedra, a dremora markynaz, these are essentially the nobles of daedric society but not nearly on the level of their princes. Slide number 8 shows a fallen snow elf riding a dangerous subterranean creature called a chaurus. Slide 9 shows a flame atronach, who can be summoned by skilled conjurers much like a storm atronach. Slide 10 showcases one of Sheogorath's servants in the realm of mania, a golden daedric saint. Slide 11 shows the rivals of the golden saints, the dark seducers who also serve Sheogorath in the realm of dementia within the shivering isles, his daedric realm. Slide 12 features Mjoll the lioness, a capable Nord shieldmaiden. Slide 13 features another daedric creature, the scamp, who are underlings for most other daedra. The last slide features the Breton blade grandmaster Jauffre who helped save the empire during the third era interregnum.
